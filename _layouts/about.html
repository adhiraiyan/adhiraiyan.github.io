I was a curious kid, I mean, so are all kids, you know what I am talking about if you have kids or if you have been around one. That is a good thing but add a lot of freedom to learn and explore into that curiosity, that might not always be a good thing, at least it wasn't in my case. Debatable, but early on, I learned, just learning things fast and going where curiosity leads is good for exploration but not so much when you want to build a strong foundation. That is why I am starting this blog.

Not to just write about fancy Machine Learning or Deep Learning techniques but to actually go to the roots and learn the basics to build a strong foundation. Since most of the current blogs just write about what is trending and miss out what is actually being used in the industry or what you actually need to learn to land a job as a Machine Learning Engineer.

The content of my blog posts are my personal notes from various sources including books, blogs posts or various articles that I read from when I was learning the subject material and what you see is a result of organizing my notes.
